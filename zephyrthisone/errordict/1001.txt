Cause: This normally happens when the DVD Drive is experiencing failure or the SATA cable is loose.

Solution: Check connections and/or replace DVD Drive.

``Additional info: Kernel could not configure DVD drive for DMA mode, ERROR_XSS_CDROM_NOT_DMA_CONFIGURED``
