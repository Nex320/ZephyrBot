Cause: GPU overheat or slim false overheat ("!Z falseOH" for more info on false overheat)

Solution: Insure console isnt suffocated, Open the console and clean and apply new thermal paste, If that doesnt work refer to !Z falseOH for more info

``Additional info: You may want to investigate the overheat further as 360's rarely naturally overheat, GPU thermal overload, ERROR_THERMAL_GPU``
