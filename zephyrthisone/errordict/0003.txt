---=====FOR FAT XBOX 360'S=====---

Cause: This is normally caused by bad capacitors.

Solution: No easy solution, Advanced solutions include recapping the motherboard.

``Additional info: VREG_GPU_PWRGD negated unexpectedly, ERROR_V_GPUCORE``


---=====FOR SLIM XBOX 360'S=====---

Cause: This is normally caused a bad voltage regulator.

Solution: No easy solution, Advanced solutions include replacing the affected regulator.

``Additional info: VREG_V3P3_PWRGD negated unexpectedly, ERROR_V_3P3``
