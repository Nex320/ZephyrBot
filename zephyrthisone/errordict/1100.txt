Cause: This can happen if your EFuse burning circut is damaged or disabled and you attempt an update.

Solution: No easy solution, Advanced solutions include Enabling/repairing the EFuse burning circut or flashing back to the previous update if you have a backup, If you want to bypass efuses modding would be in order.

``Additional info: EFuses could not be burned to current firmware version.``
