Cause: Hard disk drive (HDD) not locked.

Solution: No easy solution, Advanced solutions include Installing a modchip and or using special tools to aquire the HDD key from the xbox's TSOP and then locking said HDD via computer software. 
 
``Additional info: You should not have encountered this error unless the hard drive has been replaced previously``
