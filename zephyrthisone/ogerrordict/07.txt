Cause: Hard drive timeout / HDD took too long to become ready. This is usually caused by a dead HDD/Faulty wires.

Solution: Check the HDD wires for visible damage and check the power connector with a multimeter, If all else fails, mod the console and replace the HDD.
``Additional info: This error is most common in Xboxes with dead hard drives.``
