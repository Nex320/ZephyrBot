Cause: RAM check failed. This could be from pins on the RAM chip(s) becoming bridged, possibly from an accidental splash of solder or a failed 128MB RAM upgrade

Solution: Replace the RAM chips. this can be time consuming and is suggested to only do so with solder knowledge 

``Additional info: This error is triggered by the bootloader and as a result does not display an error code on screen. You will see the Xbox rebooting and flashing red and green lights (FRAG)``
